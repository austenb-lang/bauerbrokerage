About Bauer Brokerage

Bauer Brokerage is dedicated to helping small and medium-sized businesses secure the financing they need to operate and grow. We partner with multiple reputable lenders and programs to find funding that fits your cash flow and business goals.

Our approach

  • Client-first: We explain options and trade-offs so you choose confidently.
  • Speed: We prioritize fast decisioning for time-sensitive needs.
  • Transparency: We review fees, repayment terms, and the practical cash-flow impact.

Who we work with

Retailers, restaurants, service businesses, e-commerce, contractors, and more. If you have consistent business revenue and a plan for the funds, we’ll explore options.
